Mission

The Chair of Informatics for Medical Technologies (CIMT) at the University of Augsburg develops innovative systems and procedures for diagnostics and medical monitoring. We focus on user-friendly, powerful, and sustainable solutions, integrating advanced sensor technologies and signal processing.

"From data to diagnosis"
